Niche Profiles

General Admin

Calendar management, email triage, travel booking, data entry, basic CRM updates. The most common starting niche - high volume of clients, but also the most competitive. Differentiate with tool expertise (Notion, ClickUp) or industry focus.

$4 – $15 / hr

E-Commerce / Shopify

Product listing, inventory sync, order processing, customer support, returns/refunds. VAs who learn Shopify Liquid templating or can run Facebook Ads command the higher end of the range.

$5 – $22 / hr

Social Media Management

Content calendars, caption writing, scheduling (Buffer/Hootsuite/Later), community management, basic analytics reporting. Add short-form video editing or paid-ad management to unlock $15+.

$5 – $20 / hr

Bookkeeping / QBO

Transaction categorization, bank reconciliation, accounts receivable/payable, monthly reporting. QuickBooks Online ProAdvisor certification adds roughly $3–$5/hr to your rate. Also covers Xero and FreshBooks.

$6 – $25 / hr

Real Estate VA

MLS listing input, CRM follow-ups (Follow Up Boss, KvCORE), transaction coordination, comparative market analysis prep. High-value niche with US agents willing to pay premium rates for reliable support.

$5 – $20 / hr

Executive Assistant

Inbox zero management, board-level scheduling, travel itineraries, briefing documents, slide decks. Requires strong written English, discretion, and timezone flexibility. The "senior EA" tier overlaps with COO-level ops work.

$6 – $25 / hr

Graphic Design (Canva / Figma)

Social media graphics, pitch decks, brand kits, email headers, thumbnails. Canva-only VAs sit at the lower end; Figma/Adobe Suite proficiency pushes toward $20+. Motion graphics (After Effects, CapCut) are a premium add-on.

$5 – $22 / hr

Content / SEO Writing

Blog posts, website copy, product descriptions, keyword research, on-page SEO. Portfolio quality and niche expertise (SaaS, health, finance) drive rate differentiation. AI-aided writing has compressed entry-level rates slightly since 2024.

$5 – $20 / hr

Tech / Web Dev

WordPress builds, custom integrations (Zapier, Make), basic JavaScript/React, API work, no-code tools (Webflow, Bubble). The highest-paying VA niche - senior devs with frameworks and deployment experience can exceed $35/hr on direct contracts.

$7 – $35 / hr
